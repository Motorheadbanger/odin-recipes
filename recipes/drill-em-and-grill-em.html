Drill 'Em And Grill 'Em

Description

A recipe in the making, inspired by Deep Rock Galactic.

Equipment

  • Low tumbler glass
  • Mixing glass
  • Barspoon
  • Fine strainer
  • Cheesecloth (maybe)
  • Gas torch

Ingredients

  • Earthy/peaty whiskey
  • Something acidic (maybe, needs shaking if yes)
  • Something sweet
  • Matcha powder

Garnish

  • No garnish for now

Steps

  1. Do the matcha powder magic
  2. Strain through the cheesecloth
  3. Smoke the tumbler glass
  4. Combine everything in a mixing glass
  5. Add ice
  6. Mix
  7. Pour over a large ice cube
