A boozy tropical classic, arguably turns bad mornings better. Pi√±a Colada for grown-ups.

Equipment

  • Shaker
  • Jigger
  • Snifter or tropical cocktail glass
  • Straw
  • Grater
  • Lewis bag and mallet

Ingredients

  • 60/90/1201ml Pusser's Rum2
  • 120ml pineapple juice
  • 30ml orange juice
  • 30ml cream of coconut

Garnish

  • Nutmeg
  • Pineapple fronds

Steps

  1. Combine everything in a shaker tin
  2. Add two ice cubes, one whole, one cracked
  3. Shake for fifteen seconds
  4. Strain over crushed ice into a glass
  5. Add more crushed ice until it forms a little hill
  6. Apply pineapple fronds
  7. Grate nutmeg over the drink
  8. Put in a straw

Footnotes:

1The actual amount depends on the amount of pain to kill.

2If you use any other rum, it's legally not a Painkiller.
