A recipe in the making, inspired by Deep Rock Galactic.

Equipment

  • Collins glass
  • Shaker
  • Jigger
  • Lewis bag and mallet

Ingredients

  • Gin
  • Lime juice
  • Something sweet
  • Blue Cura√ßao or an alternative to make blue, see Kevin Kos

Garnish

  • Umbrella

Steps

  1. Combine everything in a shaker tin
  2. Add two ice cubes, one whole, one cracked
  3. Shake for fifteen seconds
  4. Strain over crushed ice into a glass
  5. Add more crushed ice until it forms a little hill
  6. Put in an umbrella and a straw
